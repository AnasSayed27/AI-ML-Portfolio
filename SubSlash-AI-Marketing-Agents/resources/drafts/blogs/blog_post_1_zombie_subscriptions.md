### What Exactly Are "Zombie Subscriptions"?

Simply put, a zombie subscription is a recurring payment for a service or product you no longer use, need, or even remember signing up for. They're insidious because they often have a low monthly cost, and with automatic renewals, they just keep charging your card without you even noticing. It’s the ultimate "set it and forget it" trap, but instead of setting yourself up for success, you’re setting yourself up to overspend.

### The Real Financial Impact: More Than Just a Few Bucks

It’s easy to dismiss a $10 or $15 monthly charge. But let’s do some quick math, shall we?

*   A $10/month subscription adds up to **$120 per year**.
*   A $20/month subscription costs you **$240 per year**.
*   Even a $5/month subscription is **$60 per year**.

Now, imagine you have three or four of these zombie subscriptions. You could easily be losing **$300, $500, or even over $1,000 a year** without realizing it! That's money that could be going towards your savings goals, paying down debt, investing for the future, or funding that amazing trip you’ve been dreaming about.
